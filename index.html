About Us

Vallalar Samarasa Suddha Sanmarka Sathya Sangam World Center

Inception History

It was started in 1983 in the old building opposite the Gnanasabha. It was started by Mr. Pattukottai Muthukumaraswamy, who worked as a post office officer in Nagapattinam. This association has members from various districts in Tamil Nadu and a few foreign countries, elected administrators and is functioning well.

Purpose

  • It is working to bring Vallalar's principles and principles to the people on a global scale in a way that is understandable to ordinary people.
  • The main goal is to eradicate hunger and to enable all people to live in spiritual unity.
  • It has created and is operating an organization that provides protection to the destitute elderly.
  • On the month-long Pusa day held every month, special annadanam is provided to the devotees who come from various places and a class on Vallalar is also conducted.
  • Every year, on Vallalar's enlightenment day, Thaipusam Thirunal and Dharma Salai Pordu Vidhanaal, food is distributed to the poor and needy. On these occasions, lectures are also given to explain Vallalar's principles.
